Programs Offered

Our Academic Programs

We offer a structured, inclusive curriculum across all levels designed to build knowledge, skills, and character.

Early Childhood Education

Nursery – Grade 3

Our early years program creates a warm, structured, and stimulating environment where young children develop foundational skills through play, exploration, and guided learning.

  • Environmental Studies
  • Arts & Craft

Basic & Secondary Level

Grade 4 – Grade 10

A comprehensive national curriculum that builds deep academic understanding and prepares students for the SEE (Secondary Education Examination) with confidence.

  • Compulsory Mathematics
  • Science & Technology
  • Social Studies & Population
  • Nepali & English
  • Optional Mathematics
  • SEE Examination Preparation

Higher Secondary — NEB

+2 Management

Our Grade 11–12 Management stream is affiliated with the National Examinations Board (NEB) and prepares students for higher education in business, finance, and commerce.

  • Business Studies
  • Economics
  • Accountancy
  • Mathematics / Finance
  • English & Nepali
  • NEB Board Examinations
Admission

How to Apply

Joining Shree Mahendra Secondary School is simple. Here's how to get started.

Step 1 — Enquire

Contact the school office or fill out the enquiry form on our Contact page. Our staff will guide you through the process.

Step 2 — Apply

Collect the admission form from the school office, complete it, and submit it along with required documents.

Step 3 — Confirm

Upon review, you will be notified of admission status and asked to complete fee payment and enrollment formalities.

Step 4 — Join

Receive your timetable, booklist, and uniform details. Welcome to the Shree Mahendra family!
