Our Story

Serving the Community Since 2016 B.S.

Shree Mahendra Secondary School was established in 2016 B.S. with a singular purpose — to bring quality education to the doorsteps of every family in Machhapuchhre-4, Kaski. What began as a modest institution in Lahachowk has grown into a school that proudly serves over 700 students from Nursery to Grade 12.

We believe that education is the most powerful tool a community has to shape its future. Every day, our 15+ dedicated teachers walk into classrooms not just to teach subjects, but to inspire young minds, build character, and nurture the potential that exists in every child.

Under the leadership of Principal Minraj Adhikari, Shree Mahendra continues to grow — adding new programs, improving facilities, and deepening our commitment to the students and families who trust us with their children's futures.

Our Foundation

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution in Gandaki Province that empowers every student with knowledge, values, and skills to become responsible, capable, and compassionate citizens of Nepal.

Our Mission

To provide inclusive, high-quality education from early childhood through higher secondary level, fostering academic excellence, moral character, and a lifelong love of learning in every student we serve.

Leadership

Principal's Message

Minraj Adhikari

Principal

"The goal of education is not to increase the amount of knowledge, but to create the possibilities for a child to invent and discover."

Dear members of our school family, it is with great pride and deep sense of responsibility that I serve as the Principal of Shree Mahendra Secondary School. Since our founding in 2016 B.S., we have strived each day to live up to the trust placed in us by the families of Machhapuchhre and the wider Kaski community.

Education is not merely about passing examinations. It is about building the whole person — instilling curiosity, discipline, empathy, and resilience. At Shree Mahendra, we have created a learning environment where students are encouraged to ask questions, explore ideas, and discover who they are and what they can contribute to the world.

I am immensely proud of our dedicated teaching staff, our supportive parent community, and above all, our students — who come to school each day with enthusiasm and determination. Together, we are building something remarkable here in Lahachowk.

I warmly invite all prospective families to visit our school, meet our teachers, and see for yourself the community we have built. The doors of Shree Mahendra are always open.

— Minraj Adhikari, Principal
