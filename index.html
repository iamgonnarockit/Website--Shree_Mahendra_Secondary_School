From the Principal's Desk

A Message of Welcome

Minraj Adhikari

Principal

"Education is not the filling of a pail, but the lighting of a fire."

Dear students, parents, and well-wishers, it is my honor and privilege to welcome you to Shree Mahendra Secondary School. Since our establishment in 2016 B.S., we have remained steadfast in our commitment to providing every child in Machhapuchhre with access to quality education that prepares them not just for examinations, but for life itself.

We believe that every student carries within them an extraordinary potential. Our role as educators is to create an environment where that potential is recognized, nurtured, and given room to flourish. With over 700 students and a team of 15 dedicated teachers, we work every day to make Shree Mahendra a place of learning, growth, and belonging.

I invite all families in our community to join us on this journey of building a better future — one student at a time.

— Minraj Adhikari, Principal
